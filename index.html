About Me

I’m a Senior Java Backend Developer with extensive experience in designing, building, and scaling high-performance backend systems using Java, Spring Boot, and microservices architecture. I specialise in developing secure, reliable, and production-ready APIs that support real-time and high-traffic applications.

I have led backend development for enterprise platforms, delivering real-time workflows using Restful APIs, WebSockets, STOMP, and message brokers. I have strong expertise in database design, transaction management, and system optimisation for mission-critical applications.

My experience includes integrating multiple payment gateways, third-party services, and external platforms, as well as building digital document signing and automation systems. I have worked extensively with cloud deployments on Microsoft Azure, focusing on scalability, security, and operational stability.

I take ownership of backend systems end-to-end from architecture and development to deployment, monitoring, and performance tuning and collaborate closely with frontend, DevOps, and product teams.
